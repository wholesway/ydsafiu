ABOUT US

We are a student and youth chapter/section of Democratic Socialists of America (DSA) and a national organization of recognized campus chapters and several hundred organizers. We are the sister chapter of Miami-DSA, centered in Florida International University. We are students organizing in our universities, colleges, and high schools to fight for the immediate change for workers and students while building our capacity to fight for more radical and structural changes.

As an organization that is following in the steps of DSA and past socialist movements, we staunchly are anti-fascist, anti-racist, anti-imperialist, and anti-capitalist.

We work with labor campaigns to organize student workers of staff, defending immigrants through campaigns for sanctuary campuses, divesting our schools from fossil fuels, anti-poverty work through local mutual aid programs in our communities, and much much more.

YDSA-FIU fights for democratic socialism through political education and active campaigns to improve the lives of working people.

OUR PROGRAMS

"Film Fridays" is our bi-weekly program hosted by our communications director, Josue, where we screen a movie with leftist themes and discuss the movie afterwards. The events include a short pre-show presentation providing context as well as a political lens to view the movie through. Join us to vote on the movie that gets screened!










"In Conversation" is our monthly crash-course political education program hosted by our Political Education chair, Jacob-Alexander. We review a piece of socialist literature, with the purpose of providing a short, comprehensible introduction to political theory. Taking the form of a socratic circle, we will engage the topic through conversation and discussion, incorporating the text into the development of our own thought. No prior knowledge is needed!






Following in the steps of past socialists movements, we believe and practice the theory of mutual aid; a form of political participation in which people take responsibility for caring for one another and changing political conditions. "Solidarity, not charity". Endeavored by our mutual-aid co-chairs, Emma and Oscar offer members a chance to work together throughout the semester in helping others by way of food, clothing, and medical drives in partnership with Miami DSA and like-minded organizations, as well as organizating participation in protests and direct political action. We hope you join us in helping out our community!
